Crockpot Burrito Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups frozen corn
  • 15 oz can pinto beans
  • 15 oz can black beans
  • 15 oz can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups white rice
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken stock
  • 3 lbs boneless chicken breast
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 4 Tbsp Green Chile
  • 1 can Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  • 1/2 Tbsp Cumin
  • 1/2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp Garlic Powder

    • Directions:

    1. Mix all ingredients in crockpot until thoroughly combined
    2. Cook on high for 3 hours, stirring occasionally
    3. (Optional) Top with shredded cheese
    4. Enjoy!
