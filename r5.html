The Best Muffin Base

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 3 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

    • Directions:

    1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Grease a muffin tin
    2. Stir flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a large bowl. Make a well in the center.
    3. Beat the egg with a fork in a small bowl. Whisk in milk and melted butter.
    4. Pour egg mixture into the well of the flour mixture, and lightly mix until combined. The batter will be lumpy. Fold in additional/optional ingredients (blueberries, chocolate chips, raspberries, cinnamon and apples, etc.)
    5. Spoon batter into the greased muffin tin (each should be 3/4 of the way full)
    6. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
    7. Allow to cool for 10 minutes after removed from the oven. Enjoy!
