Ooey Gooey Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients:

Dough

  • 3/4 cup warm (110℉) milk
  • 2 1/4 tsp quick rise or active yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 3/4 tsp salt

    • Cinnamon Filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened

    • Cream Cheese Frosting

  • 4 oz Cream Cheese, softened
  • 3 Tbsp Butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

    • Directions:

    1. Warm the milk to 110℉ (microwave for 40-45 sec). Transfer to the bowl of a standmixer and sprinkle the yeast on top. Add egg, egg yolk, granulated sugar and melted butter. Mix well until combined. Next, stir in flour and salt with a wooden spoon until dough forms. A dough hook may also be used in this step.
    2. Knead the dough with dough hook attachment for 8 minutes on medium speed. By the end of the 8 minutes, the dough should be a tot and slightly sticky ball.
    3. Transfer dough to a well oiled bowl. Cover in plastic wrap and a damp tea towel. Let rise in a warm place for 1 hr 30 min.
    4. After the dough has doubled in size, roll the dough out on a well floured surface to form a 14" x 9" rectangle. Spread the softened butter for the filling over the dough.
    5. Mix together the cinnamon and brown sugar for the filling, and sprinkle over the buttered dough.
    6. Tightly roll the dough, beginning at the 9" side of the rectangle.
    7. Carefully cut the roll into 1" sections with a sharp bread knife, or baking floss. You should get 9 rolls total.
    8. Place in a greased 9x9 baking pan. Cover in plastic and let rise for another 30-45 minutes. At this point, the dough can also be loosely covered and stored in the fridge for a long rise, for up to 2 days.
    9. While the rolls are rising, preheat the oven to 350℉. Remove plastic wrap and bake cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
    10. While baking, cream together powdered sugar, softened cream cheese, softened butter, and vanilla extract to make the frosting.
    11. When the cinnamon rolls are done baking, remove them from the oven and allow them to cool slightly for 5 minutes.
    12. Frost evenly with the prepared icing.
    13. Enjoy!
