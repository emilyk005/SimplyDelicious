Corn Tomato Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup corn kernels (cooked)
  • 5 oz diced avocado (1 medium)
  • 1 1/2 cups diced cucumber
  • 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp diced red onion
  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp Lime Juic (1 medium)
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • fresh black pepper to taste

    • Directions:

    1. Steam or grill corn until tender
    2. Let corn cool in fridge
    3. Chop all ingredients as directed
    4. Toss ingredients together
    5. Serve immediately
    6. Enjoy!
