Maple Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 3 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste

    • Directions:

    1. In a small saucepan, whisk together syrup, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper.
    2. Over medium heat bring to a simmer, stirring often.
    3. Simmer for 4 minutes or until reduced & syrupy
    4. Enjoy!
